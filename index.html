Who am I?

Hi, I'm Ariana!

I am on my way to becoming a full stack web development programmer. I started my program at Lambda Schools in the beginning of August 2019 and I am due to graduate in Febuary of 2021. This journey is extremely invigorating and I look forward to seeing where it takes me! Outside of programming I enjoy spending my time making 'Happy Mail', mixed media art, reading, and hiking. Nothing makes me feel more at peace than being surrounded by nature and I hope one day to own property where I can live the rural lifestyle I dream of.
