My Work Model

The bellow sections are my 6 key focuses for success

Communication

Communication is key to any project. It helps to keep everyone on the same page with clear goals and the teams vision in mind. Good communication skills are also a very vital component of anyone aspiring to become a great leader or programer.

Repetition

Learning isn't a one and done subject, it's being able to do it over and over again with accuracy, focus, and a resolve to never give up that'll grant you the ability to master it.

Big Picture

Nothing worth while ever comes easy, that's why it's always important to remember the bigger picture when you find yourself struggling on the little things.

Honest Working

To take someone's work, is to cheat yourself from learning. By applying yourself through repetition and hard studying, and never being afraid to ask for help, you will set yourself up in a position to succeed as well as learn from your failures.

Equipping Yourself

Being prepared and equipped for difficult times is a wise way to better position yourself for success and stay innovative. Wise seeking after the right goals will find you better prepared for the challenge ahead.

Success

There will be both failure and success in life, but if you learn from failures, equipping yourself through repetition of honest work, and stay focused on the big picture, you will find that communicates into success more often than not.
