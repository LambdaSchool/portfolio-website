About Me

I was born and raised in the beautiful state of Maine where I worked for many years in banking and insurance. After many years in an office setting I felt a calling to take more control of my earning potential and moved into sales. Having spent many years in sales and feeling warn out from the daily grind I felt the pressure to start persuing something that gave me joy and I could find passion in. That is when I decided to go back to school and learn how to code. From a very young age when PC's were uncommon in most households and computer were controlled by a command line I was fascinated! Here I am starting a new journey and finding happiness in learning something that I have always deeply enjoyed.
