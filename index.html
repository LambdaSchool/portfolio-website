Hi. I'm Greg Cushing.

I'm an Arborist transitioning to a coder who loves music and video games.

Learn about what I do

Here's all the stuff I do.

Certified Arborist

I have been climbing trees and doing tree work for 11 years. I have been a Certified Arborist for 8 years. I was trained by world class arborists at Limbwalker Tree Service and have been working at Cave Hill Cemetery and Arboretum for the last 3 years. I am passionate about conserving the canopy here in Louisville, Kentucky which is one of the nations fastest growing heat islands. My specialization is climbing large, hard to access trees for pruning and conservation practices.

Coding and Web Development

I am currently enrolled in Lambda School's part time Web Development Course. I am excited to challenge myself and learn these new skills which I hope to one day combine with my experience and passion for arboriculture. I have always been interested in computers and technology, but this is my first foray into learning the technical arts.

Music Making

I got my first drum kit when I was 13 and have been playing ever since. I began making electronic music about 7 years ago. Synthesizers, drum machines, sequences, and all manner of elecronic music instruments fascinate me to no end. I spend days just resreaching the equipment. Sometimes I'll actually turn mine on and make MUSIC with the stuff! Drums are something I have chops with and can jam with other people. Synthesizer music is more of a delightfully lonesome and private experience.
