About

I started working with HTML, CSS, and PHP in 2008 during the summer of my sophomore year. I taught my webmastering class the following two years, made a website, and never did anything with it. Shortly after, I joined the army, went to South Korea, and realized that computer languages aren't the only type that I'm good at learning.

Now, here I am - engaging with a delightful school known as Lambda!
